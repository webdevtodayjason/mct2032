Installation Guide

1

Connect Device

Connect your Waveshare ESP32-S3-LCD-1.47 to your computer via USB-C cable.

2

Click Install

Click the "Install Firmware" button above. Your browser will prompt for serial port access.

3

Select Port

Choose the serial port for your ESP32 device from the browser dialog.

4

Wait for Flash

The firmware will be installed automatically. This takes about 2-3 minutes.
